Who Are We?

Mission Statement

  1. Our mission is to voice & empower the community of Holloway actively.
  2. Create platforms for dialogue > towards positive change, unity, & empowerment of us individuals in Holloway.
  3. Ensure that your voice & needs are heard and addressed.
  4. Create a safe space where every resident, worker, and community member can engage in open dialogue.
  5. Amplify new individual perspectives on how Holloway is evolving & changing - to shape a brighter & more inclusive future.
  6. Connect Holloway with pre-existing community support groups.
