Who Are We?

At Represent Holloway we aim to build platforms to bridge the gap between the people of Holloway to various ways of engaging with their local community to build a better, more inclusive, and equal Holloway for everyone.

Mission Statement:

  1. Our mission is to voice & empower the communities of Holloway
  2. Create platforms towards positive change, unity, & empowerment of the individuals in Holloway
  3. Create a safe and inclusive environment where every resident, worker, and community member can have their needs heard and addressed.
  4. Amplify new individual perspectives to shape a brighter & more inclusive future for Holloway
  5. Connect Holloway with pre-existing community support groups

The Community Support Directory:

The Community Support Directory is a community-led & updated directory for the public

Community -> methods to engage & help their community

Community -> services available for them

The Community Support Directory is open-source & designed to be able to be adapted by anyone to adopt into their community, without prior knowledge of coding and easily modified by editing a spreadsheet.
