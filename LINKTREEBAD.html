Immerse yourself in the North London based rapper BETI’s world and his debut EP - NEVER SCARED AGAIN, following his lastest single “Bad Ways Pt.2”. The 24-year-old Parisian artist blends old school sampling and Grime drum breaks, bridging the London Alternative sounds and French lyricism.
