My first solo release: Untitled Vol.2 is 17 curated Beats from 2020 - 2022, I'm always working on new music, and I see this beat tape as a by-product of my life: what I've felt, what inspires me, and the vibes I set.

Looking back at it, it's one of my favourite projects I've produced: It's a 25 minute sonic soundtrack to a very special period of growth for me and the people around me.
