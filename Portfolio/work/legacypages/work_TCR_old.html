A documentary on how the British Highstreet has changed. Watch to join in on a conversation I had with a shopkeeper of one of the last remaining independent electronic stores on Tottenham Court Road, a road which was once a world famous centre for consumer electronics. Online shopping has played a large role in the change of the highstreet, but we also discuss about how the greed of landlords, the government, or other powers have resulted in the streets we live in today.
